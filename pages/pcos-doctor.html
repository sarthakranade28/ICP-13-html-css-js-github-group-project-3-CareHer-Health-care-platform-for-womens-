PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a hormonal disorder affecting ovulation, metabolism, and overall reproductive health. Early medical care reduces complications.

  • Irregular or missed periods
  • Excess facial or body hair
  • Weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Acne & oily skin
  • Hair thinning or hair fall
  • Mood swings & anxiety
