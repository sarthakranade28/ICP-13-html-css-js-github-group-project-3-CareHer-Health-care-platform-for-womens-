Overview & Basic Information About Body Mass Index

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement using height and weight to estimate whether a person has a healthy body weight.

Why BMI Matters

BMI is used to identify possible health risks related to underweight, overweight, and obesity.

Limitations of BMI

BMI does not account for muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal weight
25 – 29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obesity
